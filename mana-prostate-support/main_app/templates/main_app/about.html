About Mana Prostate Support

Mana Prostate Support is dedicated to providing comprehensive information and support for individuals and families affected by prostate cancer in New Zealand. Our mission is to empower men with the knowledge they need to navigate their prostate health journey and offer a community of support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a trusted resource where men can find up-to-date, relevant information about prostate health and connect with others facing similar challenges. We aim to raise awareness about prostate cancer prevention, early detection, and treatment options.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster a community where every man in New Zealand has access to the knowledge and resources needed to take control of his prostate health. We envision a future where early detection rates improve, treatment is widely understood, and support is readily available for those who need it.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness of prostate health.
  • Provide a comprehensive resource for men and their families about prostate cancer.
  • Build a support network for individuals living with prostate cancer.
